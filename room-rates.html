General Cancellation Policy

Cancellation more than 28 days before scheduled arrival £25.00 per person to cover administrations costs. Cancellation 28 days or less before scheduled arrival. If we re-sell the accommodation in full £25.00 per person to cover administration costs. If we fail to resell the accommodation previously booked or any part thereof you will be liable for the full costs of the original booking less any monies recovered by us by way of a partial resale to offset these costs. Date of cancellation is the date we receive your notice of cancellation which we must be given in writing. We would strongly advise travel insurance to minimise any cost to your self should you need to cancel. Payment may be made by, Visa or Mastercard Credit or Debit, Prepayment by bank transfer (preferred option for groups), Cash.
